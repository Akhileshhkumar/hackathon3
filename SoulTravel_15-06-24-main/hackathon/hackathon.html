Hackathon

This track is designed for second-, third-, and fourth-year students who are ready to tackle real-world problems. Participants will work on solving problem statements provided by us, bringing their technical knowledge and problem-solving abilities to the forefront. InnoVortex 2.O is not just a competition; it's a celebration of innovation, creativity, and collaboration. Here, students from various backgrounds come together to share ideas, reimagine possibilities, and create solutions that could shape the future. Join us in this journey of exploration and invention, where every idea has the potential to make a difference!
